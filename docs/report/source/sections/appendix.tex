\chapter{Appendix: Data Dictionaries and Sample Metrics}
\label{ch:appendix}

This appendix collects schemas and sample production aggregates referenced in the main chapters. Percentages in Tables~\ref{tab:appendix-funnel}--\ref{tab:appendix-modality} are from a 30-day UAT/prod window (April--May 2026) and are illustrative rather than contractual SLAs.

\section{Message Document Schema}
\label{sec:appendix-messages}

Each dialogue turn is stored at \code{conversations/\{cid\}/messages/\{mid\}}. Table~\ref{tab:message-schema} is the authoritative field dictionary for analytics jobs and dashboard exporters.

\tablesetup
\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{\code{messages} sub-document field dictionary.}
\label{tab:message-schema}
\small
\begin{tabular}{|p{2.8cm}|p{1.6cm}|p{8.1cm}|}
\hline
\TblHead{Field} & \TblHead{Type} & \TblHead{Description} \\
\hline
\texttt{conversationId} & string & Parent conversation key \\
\hline
\texttt{authorId} & string & Firebase UID or \texttt{system} \\
\hline
\texttt{role} & string & \texttt{user}, \texttt{model}, \texttt{tool} \\
\hline
\texttt{content} & string & Normalised text transcript (no raw audio) \\
\hline
\texttt{modality} & string & Channel that produced the turn \\
\hline
\texttt{tokenCount} & int & Optional per-turn token estimate \\
\hline
\texttt{createdAt} & timestamp & Server timestamp (Firestore) \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
